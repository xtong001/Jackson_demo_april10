Hello, I'm Jackson

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm a 25-year-old ocean wanderer and sunset collector who believes life's best moments happen between plans. This is where I share my journeys, both across continents and through the winding paths of everyday curiosity.

Who I Am

Background: Born between waves and mountains, I've been splitting my time between coastal towns and the open road since I could walk. Currently based wherever the surf forecast looks promising.

Passions: Ocean conservation, spontaneous road trips, and documenting the poetry in ordinary moments. I'm most alive when knee-deep in saltwater or scribbling thoughts in weathered notebooks.

Goals: To live intentionally without overplanning, protect our oceans, and inspire others to embrace the beauty of unscripted living.

What Moves Me

Personal Philosophy

I believe in saltwater therapy, the wisdom of tides, and that the best conversations happen when we're not trying to impress anyone. Life's too short for perfect grammar and filtered sunsets - I'll take raw, messy authenticity every time.
